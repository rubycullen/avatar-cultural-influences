Society & Belief Systems

Images from Avatar

The Asian Inspiration

The inmensity and diversity of the Earth Kingdom populace is very similar to that of China. People of a myriad of different skin tones, facial features, and cultures make up the Earth Kingdom population. The disparity between economic classes in the Earth Kingdom is also similar to China. In both nations, the small upper class is far richer than the huge lower class. In the Earth Kingdom's capital city of Ba Sing Se, there are literal walls between the different castes—the Upper Ring contains all the nation's richest and most privileged, including the Earth King and his advisors, while the Outer Ring has all the nation's poorest—refugees, artists, peddlers, living all crammed together.
